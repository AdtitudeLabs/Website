How & What we do?

We have a simple 5 step process to get this going for a long-term association

Onboarding

We will meet in-person or invite you over an online meeting. We will ask comprehensive questions to understand about both your personal and professional life.

Because we want to see the world in your shoes, so we can share the story better.

It is a causal Interaction between all of us. Don’t worry, you secrets are safe with us.

Profile Dissection

Our expert team will analyze your profile.  

We will plan on optimization. This includes a Killer Headline, Strong Banner Image and an Engaging bio based on your persona.

This happens in the next 48-72 hours.

Content Strategy & Calendar

Based on all the inputs from your side, we will set out the tone and strategy for the upcoming month.

All this will shared in the text format and a proper content calendar will be prepared.

This includes funnels, topics and tone of the content.

Review, Feedback & Approve

The content calendar will be sent out in Notion along with the respective creatives for your approval on every Friday Evening.

You can use the weekend to read through the content, review and approve it.

All the approved content will go out from Monday as per the calendar.

Progress Report

We have an internal meeting every week to analyze the performance of the content. We will understand what works best and what doesn’t for your persona.

Revise Strategies, revamp content and reply to DMs based on feedback.

We will share a monthly report every 30 days.
